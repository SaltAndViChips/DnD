Gluttony is a black figure that visits adventurers on the road in their dreams.

Gluttony will appear as a shadow where it should not be, and slowly approach the character until directly looming over them.  If a player character does not eat while in [[Greatmarsh]], on the next long rest. The character must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be given [[Eternal Hunger| Famine]].
