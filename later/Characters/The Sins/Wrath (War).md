Wrath is a female Warforged made from gleaming bright metal stained from an endless amount of blood and gore she has created.

Wrath works with [[Death]] to prepare the souls of those who have altered their fate and should be dead. Wrath despises resurrection in all forms, being solely programed to kill anything that does not fit on their approved lifeforms list.
