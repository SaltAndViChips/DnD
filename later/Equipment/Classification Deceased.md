melee weapon (martial, halberd)

- Category: Items
- Damage: 1d10
- Damage Type: Slashing
- Properties: Heavy, Reach, Two-Handed

Classification: Deceased 

You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, the weapon ignores resistance to slashing damage.

Proficiency with a halberd allows for adding proficiency bonus to attack.

**Classification: Focus** 
This can be used as a spell focus (Staff)

**Classification: Attunement** 
The attunement of this item requires the sacrifice of an innocent life who has strayed from their fated path. (the victim can be anyone of good alignment either another player or an NPC. The sacrifice must be killed by this weapon) by soaking the blade in their blood. Once attuned to a wielder this weapon can only bond to a different wielder once its current wielder is dead.

**Classification: Fated Life**
On your turn you may use an action to check if a target within 30ft has avoided their fate and should be dead. This action may be used once per long rest equal to the characters level.
