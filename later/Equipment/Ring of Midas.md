Attuned to the user upon wearing.

When worn it melts and covers the hand in solid gold, while still functioning as a normal the user also becomes immune to all curses related to gold or jewels. Mainly [[Dragons Madness]]

The user weighs more depending on their size:
Small: +5lbs
Medium: +15lbs
Large: +20lbs
Huge: +25lbs

When in water, user must make an athletics check to remain afloat, or sink equal to the weight gained once per turn.
