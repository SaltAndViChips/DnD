The robes of sloth are incredibly comfortable and durable robes belonging to [[Sloth (Lot)|Sloth]].

These robes are attuned to a user when gifted to them by [[Sloth (Lot)| Sloth]]. 


### While attuned you gain the following effects: 
Sleeping while wearing these robes provides the benefit of a long rest for every short rest, along with Sanctuary.
User is resistant to scrying and gains advantage on spells that effect sleep.
The spell Sleep is twice as effective while attuned. 
The user always has a great night sleep, and is immune to nightmares.
